Types of Soil

  • Alluvial Soil: Good for wheat and rice
  • Black Soil: Ideal for cotton
  • Red Soil: Suitable for millets
  • Laterite Soil: Used for tea and coffee

Suitable Crops

Alluvial Soil → Rice, Wheat

Black Soil → Cotton

Red Soil → Groundnut

Laterite Soil → Tea, Coffee

Fertilizers Information

  • Organic: Compost, manure
  • Chemical: Urea, NPK
  • Bio Fertilizers: Rhizobium
